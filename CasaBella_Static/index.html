Our Community

Past the stately oak-lined entrance, tranquil lakes, and elegant fountains—beyond the privacy of a secure gated entry—lies the prestigious community of Casa Bella.

Designed for residents with refined tastes, Casa Bella showcases luxury custom homes built by some of Brevard County’s most acclaimed builders, including Joe DiPrima Homes, Chris Burton Homes, Scallero Homes, Bayswater by Dude Braselton, and Arthur Rutenberg Homes.

Each home is thoughtfully crafted with indulgent features like spa-inspired bathrooms, gourmet chef’s kitchens, marble flooring, and expansive open-concept designs—elevating everyday living to a luxurious experience.

LOCATION

Situated just north of the Pineda Causeway, in Melbourne, Florida, Casa Bella is located conveniently to Interstate 95. With an hour drive to Orlando and just 10 minutes to the beaches of the Atlantic ocean, and the Town of Viera. Casa Bella is a perfect place to call home and allows for sunning on the beach, shopping all day, or great dining out.

  • 10 minutes to public beach
  • 10 minutes to The Avenue Viera
  • 45 min to ORL INT

Amenities

Offering a safe and family-inviting neighborhood was obviously the goal of our community’s design team, featuring a private fenced pathway to the nearby elementary throughout the community and a secure playground, housing top-of-the-line equipment. Additional amenities include lighted tennis courts and impeccably designed and maintained grounds.

  • fenced path to elementary school
  • secure playground
  • lighted tennis courts
  • beautifully maintained public commons
  • full court basketball
  • newly rennovated clubhouse (2025)
